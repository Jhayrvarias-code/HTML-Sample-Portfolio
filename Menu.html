Adobong Sitaw

Ingridients

Preparation

  • 500g sitaw (string beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the sitaw and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Pour in the vinegar and soy sauce. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add water, cover, and cook until the sitaw is tender (about 10-15 minutes).
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
